About Our Rescue

We are a volunteer‑run cat rescue based in Western Australia, dedicated to helping abandoned, stray, and surrendered cats find safe and loving homes.

Our mission is simple: rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome. We work closely with foster carers, vets, and the community to ensure every cat receives the care they deserve.

Thank you for supporting our work — together, we’re giving cats a second chance.
